Categorized Scrolling Booklet of Data Boxes

General Information

For my reusable component, I have made a scrolling booklet of data that allows the categorization of JSON objects by a specific attribute selected by the HTML coder. Additionally, the information shown on both the front and back of the boxes are chosen by the developer. Therefore, there is plenty of control for the person programming, allowing for a useful reusable component. To use it, once must have four different input parameters: "jsonList", which is a list of JSON objects parsed to an array, "headingProperty", which is one of the attributes of the JSON object that is used for categorizing, "frontInfo", which is which elements a developer wants on the front of the card, and "backInfo", which is the attributes for the back of the card. When hovering over the cards, they have a flipping animation, and switch colors. When you click on of the headers, it will only show you the booklets that fall under that specific category.
